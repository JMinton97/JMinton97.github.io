photos

I got my first camera free with a magazine when I was 10. It was digital, but without a persistent memory, so if you swung it too hard by the strap (as 10 year olds do) the AAA batteries came out you lost all your photos. Five cameras later I'm just as enthusiastic but I treat them slightly better now.

I mainly take pictures of nice landscapes but have a wide experience of styles so feel free to get in touch! I probably wouldn't back myself to photograph your wedding but am open to other suggestions!
